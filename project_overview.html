Project Overview: Cozy Corner Cafe Website

Project Overview:

Application Description and Purpose: This web application will serve as the online presence for Cozy Corner Cafe, a local cafe in Charlotte, NC. Its purpose is to provide information about the cafe to potential and existing customers, including its menu, story, contact details, and customer testimonials. The website aims to enhance the cafe's visibility, improve communication with customers, and foster a sense of community.

Intended Users of the Website: The intended users are primarily local residents of Charlotte and visitors who are looking for a cafe to enjoy coffee, pastries, and light bites. This includes individuals looking for a place to relax, work, or meet friends. Secondary users might include potential employees or local businesses interested in partnerships.

Overview of the Content of the Website: The website will include information about the cafe's menu with descriptions and potentially images of food and drinks, the story and values of the cafe, contact information including address, phone number, email, and operating hours, and a section for customer testimonials to build trust and social proof.

Page Design:

For each of the five required pages (Home and four others), here is a breakdown of their design:

1. Home Page (index.html)

  • Purpose: Introduction to Cozy Corner Cafe with highlights and CTA to explore the site.
  • Audience: All visitors.
  • Content: Hero image, welcome text, highlights, "View Menu" button.
  • Data Entry Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links/Buttons: Nav links, "View Menu" button.
  • Actions: Navigation.
  • Notes: Emphasize warmth and welcoming visuals.

2. Menu Page (menu.html)

  • Purpose: Show available food and drink items.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Menu items, prices, images.
  • Data Entry Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links/Buttons: Nav links.
  • Actions: Navigation.
  • Notes: Mobile-friendly design.

3. About Us Page (about.html)

  • Purpose: Tell the story and values of the cafe.
  • Audience: Curious or loyal customers.
  • Content: Narrative, photos, history.
  • Data Entry Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links/Buttons: Nav links, social links.
  • Actions: Navigation and external links.
  • Notes: Personal tone, warm design.

4. Contact Page (contact.html)

  • Purpose: Help customers contact the cafe.
  • Audience: Customers with questions or directions.
  • Content: Address, map, phone, email, hours, contact form.
  • Data Entry Fields: Name, Email, Message.
  • Validation: JavaScript for required fields and email format.
  • Links/Buttons: Nav links, "Send Message" button.
  • Actions: Form submission (client-side), navigation.
  • Notes: Address may be a clickable map link.

5. Testimonials Page (testimonials.html)

  • Purpose: Build trust through customer feedback.
  • Audience: New customers looking for social proof.
  • Content: Real or placeholder customer testimonials, names, optionally star ratings.
  • Data Entry Fields: None in this version (future versions may allow submissions).
  • Validation: Not applicable.
  • Links/Buttons: Nav links.
  • Actions: Navigation only.
  • Notes: Can include a carousel or simple layout.
